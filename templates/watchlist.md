# Watchlist (master queue)

The watchlist is where everything dated for the future lives — deadlines, ideas, blockers, observations. It never goes in the day file. When an item's date arrives, it enters the morning plan by itself. The assistant maintains it; you own it.

## §0. Milestones / top-level goals

Big outcomes you're working toward. Linked to the goals in `base.md` §5.

- [ ] [Milestone 1]: target date, what done means

## §1. Active / deadlines (next 2 weeks)

Anything with a concrete date or deadline for today-to-15 days. These surface in the morning plan automatically when their date arrives.

- [ ] [DD.MM]: [task / deadline]
- [ ] [DD.MM]: [task / deadline]

## §2. Project queue (ready, backlog, ideas)

Tasks and projects without immediate dates. Ordered roughly by readiness.

- [ ] [project]: [short description, one line]
- [ ] [idea]: [one line]

### Park (stalled, reconsider)

Items stuck ≥2 cycles without movement. Kept as reference until you decide "still live" or move to §6.

- (none)

## §3. Blockers

External obstacles or decisions waiting on others.

- [ ] [blocker]: waiting on [who/what], by [date if known]

## §4. Observations / patterns (material)

Hypotheses, recurring patterns, mismatches worth tracking. Logged but not acted on directly.

- [observation]: [one line, no action required]

## §5. Small fixes (typos, quick tasks)

One-liners, bugs, minor improvements. Low energy or in-between time.

- [ ] [small task]

## §6. Archive (completed)

Closed items with completion date. History of done.

- [DD.MM]: [completed task / goal]
